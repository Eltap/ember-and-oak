Why Ember & Oak

We started in a tiny apartment kitchen, testing scent blends until the neighbors started asking where the candle shop was. Every batch is still poured by hand, in small runs, so we can actually stand behind the quality.

  • Cotton wicks, no lead or zinc cores
  • Reusable glass jars
  • 40+ hour burn time per candle
  • Scented, tested, and approved by our (very patient) neighbors
