Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a candle burn?

Each candle is rated for 40+ hours of burn time, tested with 3-4 hour burns at a time for the most even wax pool.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship within the US only. International shipping is on the roadmap.

Are your candles safe for pets?

Our soy wax and cotton wicks are non-toxic, but as with any candle, keep lit candles out of reach of curious paws and away from anything flammable.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes — reach out through the form above with "Wholesale" as the topic and we'll follow up with minimums and pricing.
